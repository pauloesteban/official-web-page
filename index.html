About

At present, I work as a remote software engineer for US-based companies and several clients around the globe.

I earned a BSc degree with a major in electrical engineering and telecommunications at Escuela Politécnica del Ejército (now Universidad de las Fuerzas Armadas – ESPE). Furthermore, I was awarded with a MSc with merit in Sound and Music Computing at Queen Mary University of London.

My current research interests include digital signal processing, artificial intelligence, and music cognition.
